Our Story

How Ceylon Organic came to life

Ceylon Organic was born from a deep appreciation for Sri Lanka's rich spice heritage and a commitment to sustainable farming practices. Our journey began in 2015 when our founder, Dinidu Galahitiyawa, returned to his ancestral village in central Sri Lanka after studying sustainable agriculture abroad.

Witnessing the unique quality of spices grown in Sri Lanka's mineral-rich soil and perfect climate, yet seeing small-scale farmers struggle to reach global markets, Dinidu envisioned a company that would bridge this gap. He partnered with local farming communities to create a business model that prioritizes quality, sustainability, and fair compensation.

Today, Ceylon Organic works with over 200 family farms across Sri Lanka, helping them adopt organic farming practices while bringing their exceptional products to spice enthusiasts worldwide. Every product in our collection carries the essence of Sri Lanka's spice-growing tradition, maintained through generations of expertise and now shared with you.

Our Process

From garden to your doorstep

1

Cultivation

Our partner farmers cultivate spices using traditional organic methods, nurturing plants in Sri Lanka's ideal growing conditions.

2

Harvesting

Spices are hand-harvested at peak ripeness to ensure maximum flavor and aromatic qualities.

3

Processing

Using traditional methods combined with modern safety standards, we process spices to preserve their essential oils and flavors.

4

Quality Control

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing for purity, potency, and consistency before being approved.

5

Packaging

Spices are carefully packaged in small batches to maintain freshness and shipped directly to customers worldwide.
